• Electricity consumption is the amount of electrical energy consumed by households, businesses, and industries.
  • The unit of measurement for electricity consumption is the kilowatt-hour (kWh).
  • The United States is the world's largest consumer of electricity, followed by China and India.
  • The majority of electricity consumed globally is generated by burning fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and natural gas.
  • The transportation sector accounts for a small fraction of global electricity consumption, as most vehicles still rely on fossil fuels for energy.
